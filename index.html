Our Facilities

The school has already allocated a budget for a SPORTS COMPLEX, LIBRARY at HALLWAY.

SPORT COMPLEX

The sports complex provides a dynamic space for physical activities and athletic development. Equipped with courts and fields for various sports such as basketball, volleyball, and badminton, it serves as a hub for fitness, teamwork, and school spirit. The facility hosts regular sports events and training sessions, fostering a well-rounded education.

LIBRARY

The college library is a haven for knowledge and research, offering a wide array of books, digital resources, and study materials. With a comfortable and quiet environment, it is ideal for studying, group discussions, and academic exploration. Computer stations and internet access are available to support modern learning needs.

HALLWAY

The hallways at Bestlink College are designed to be clean, spacious, and well-lit, ensuring a safe and pleasant environment for students and staff. They are adorned with inspiring quotes and educational displays to motivate learners as they move between classes. Hallways also serve as venues for interactive bulletin boards showcasing school events, achievements, and announcements.
